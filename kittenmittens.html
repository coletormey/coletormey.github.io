What are Kitten Mittens?

Is your cat making TOO much NOISE all the time? Is your
cat constantly stomping around, driving you crazy? Is
your cat clawing at your furniture? Think there's no answer?
You're so stupid! There is: Kitten Mittens.

They are unlike any other mitten on the market. Kitten Mittens
are designed to fit snugly on the paw of any cat. They are cut from
regular mittens and re-stitched into a more cat friendly form.

An Idea, Born Through Necessity

If you have ever owned a cat, or know someone who has, then you're probably already
aware of that one bothersome thing cats do all too often: walk around.

They are always moving around, putting one paw in front of another, creating an
endless stream of deafening sound that plagues your living space. This ceaseless
cycle of torturous noise would continue for the rest of time were it not for one brilliant
mind: Charlie Kelly.

The year was 2009. Being surrounded by cats on a daily basis, Charlie took it
upon himself to find an effective solution to put and end to the trouble being
caused by our feline friends. Thus, Kitten Mittens were conceived.

Our patented mitten design works to cover the entire paw, and not just
the annoying claw parts. The footsteps of any cat fitted with a pair of Kitten Mittens
will be rendered silent.* You'll be able to relax and unwind without the
bothersome pitter-patter of malicious feet.






*CAUTION: If you believe your cat to have ill intentions towards you, DO NOT
put Kitten Mittens on him/her, as their newfound silence will give them the upper hand.
